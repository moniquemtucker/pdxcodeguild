Career + Life Coaching

This page describes the general steps of my coaching process, although when we work together we'll develop a fully customized plan that focuses on just the areas most important to you.

Results can come more quickly than you might expect. Many of my clients are able to get clarity and results they want in just 4 to 6 sessions. Click here to get started with a free consultation.

Clarifying What You Want

First, we'll help you clarify the direction you want to move towards in your life and/or career, and we'll use that clarity to set concrete coaching goals. For example, clients may feel they have too many options to consider and don't know where to begin.

In these cases we use an evidence-based approach wherein we mine past experiences and pinpoint clues about 'what works best' in the client. From this evidence we learn what is inessential and which options can be subtracted.

Leveraging Your Strengths

Next, we'll explore what intrinsically motivates you and how you can best use your strengths and resources to get to where you want to go. During this phase we may discover untapped strengths, unrealized resources, and a greater appreciation of what you have available and how to deploy yourself.

The aim is that you'll become better equipped with knowledge of your strengths and internal resources that is specific to you -- information that, we'll come to see, has highly practical applications.

Making it Happen

Finally, we'll develop and implement a plan of small, manageable actions and concrete commitments to get you from here to there.

We'll consider potential obstacles and how to manage them, and set you on the path to achieving what you want for your life and/or career.
